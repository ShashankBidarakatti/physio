1. Elbow Extension

  • 1. Lie on your back with a weight in your hand.
  • 2. Raise and extend your arm over your shoulder, holding the elbow with the other hand.
  • 3. Slowly lower your hand towards your head by bending the elbow.
  • 4. Make sure to keep the upper arm perpendicular to the floor.
  • 5. Extend the elbow and repeat.

2. Elbow Flexion

  • 1. Place your arm at your side, elbow straight, hand in neutral (palm inward) and holding a weight.
  • 2. Keeping your elbow at your side, bend your arm up while keeping the hand in neutral.
  • 3. Return to the starting position and repeat.
