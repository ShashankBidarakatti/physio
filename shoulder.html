1. Scaption Strengthening

  • 1. Stand with weights in your hands, arms straight at your sides, thumbs up and your shoulders down and back.
  • 2. Raise your arms in a 45 degree angle up to parallel.
  • 3. Return to the starting position and repeat.

2. Assisted Shoulder Flexion

  • 1. Clasp your hands in front of your body.
  • 2. Raise them over your head by letting your stronger side guide and assist your weaker side.

3. Assisted Shoulder

  • 1. Stand in front of a wall and place the hand of the affected arm on the wall in front of you.
  • 2. With your fingers, climb up the wall as high as you can and come back to the starting position.
  • 3. Repeat.

4. Side Lying External Rotation

  • 1. Lie on your side with a weight in your top hand, elbow bent to 90 degrees.
  • 2. Place a rolled towel between your arm and your side.
  • 3. Rest your head on your free arm.
  • 4. Externally rotate the arm to lift the weight up.
  • 5. Keep your body still; do not turn the trunk to assist the movement.

5. AAROM External Rotation

  • 1. Stand up against a wall.
  • 2. Position your affected arm on the wall close to your body, elbow bent 90 degrees, palm up.
  • 3. Push the affected arm out to the side toward the wall as far as possible by pushing on the stick with the good hand.
  • 4. Maintain the position and slowly return to the middle.
  • 5. Repeat.
