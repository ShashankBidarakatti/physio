1. Cervical Nod

  • 1. Sit down on a chair.
  • 2. Lift your breastbone up slightly and draw your shoulder blades back to obtain a good posture.
  • 3. Nod your chin down as far as you can without involving the neck-only the head should move.
  • 4. Hold the nod at that point for the recommended time. Then relax and return your head to the starting position.

2. Left Side Head Bending

  • 1. In a sitting or standing position, bend your head to the left as far as possible. Think about putting your ear on top of your shoulder, without any elevation of the shoulder.
  • 2. Modify the amount of chin retraction (tucking your chin) in order to centralize the pain.
